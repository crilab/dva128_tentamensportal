Teachers often organize hotdogs and drinks for school trips. Because calculating the required supplies manually is time-consuming and prone to errors, you will write a script to automate this process.

Before each trip, teachers ask students to choose from four specific meal options. Your script must handle the math for them by using four distinct input() statements—one for each individual category—asking how many students selected:

- 2 hotdogs
- 3 hotdogs
- 2 vegan hotdogs
- 3 vegan hotdogs

Based on the students' choices, your script must calculate and display:

- The number of regular hotdog packages needed (8 hotdogs per package)
- The number of vegan hotdog packages needed (4 vegan hotdogs per package)
- The total number of drinks needed (1 per student)
- The total cost of the entire order

The prices for the items are as follows:

| Product        | Cost                |
| -------------- | ------------------- |
| Regular hotdog | 20.95:- per package |
| Vegan hotdog   | 34.95:- per package |
| Drink          | 13.95:- each        |
